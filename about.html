Software Engineer & Entrepreneur from Kerala

I'm Faris Ahammed Ali, a passionate software engineer and the founder of Qumiq, a technology company based in Kerala, India. With extensive experience in full-stack development, mobile app development, and software architecture, I specialize in creating innovative digital solutions for businesses across Kochi, Trivandrum, Calicut, and throughout Kerala.

Professional Background

Over the years, I've worked with leading companies including Lyracorp Private Limited, Necttos Private Limited, and Brototype, where I've led teams in building enterprise-level ERP systems, automation software for educational institutions, and full-stack web applications. My expertise spans across modern web technologies including React, Node.js, Express, Next.js, React Native, and database systems like MongoDB and PostgreSQL.

Qumiq - Revolutionizing Hospitality Technology in Kerala

In January 2025, I founded Qumiq, a technology startup focused on developing smart, real-time solutions for the hospitality industry. Our flagship product, Scannserve, is a QR-based SaaS platform that streamlines restaurant operations by enabling customers to scan a QR code, place orders digitally, and have Kitchen Order Tickets (KOT) sent directly to the kitchen—reducing wait times and improving customer experience.
